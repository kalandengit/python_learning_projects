# Product Hunt Launch Kit

## Product name
DocuPilot AI

## Tagline
Turn screenshots into beautiful documentation in minutes.

## Short description
DocuPilot AI helps teams upload product screenshots and instantly generate editable guides, SOPs, help center articles, onboarding docs, and release notes.

## FAQ

### Who is this for?
SaaS companies, agencies, product managers, QA teams, support teams, educators, and technical writers.

### What files are supported in beta?
The beta focuses on screenshot uploads. Video, browser recording, and desktop recording are planned.

### Does AI replace technical writers?
No. It creates the first draft and structure. Human review remains essential.

### Can I export my documentation?
Yes. Markdown, HTML, and PDF export are included.

### Is it enterprise ready?
The architecture prepares for enterprise features, but SSO, SCIM, DPA, and SOC 2 are roadmap items.
