# Legal Notes

The included privacy and terms pages are implementation placeholders for beta-readiness. They are not legal advice.

Before accepting production customers, review:
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Data Processing Addendum
- Cookie Policy
- AI provider data-processing terms
- Security commitments
- Refund policy
- Acceptable use policy

Enterprise roadmap:
- DPA
- SOC 2 readiness documentation
- Vendor risk questionnaire
- Data retention controls
- SSO and SCIM
- Audit log export
